HER

She's the girl who can't fully communicate her feelings
even with the people she loves the most
Every time she starts to talk it feels like a word clot forms in her head
then she's suddenly confused on what to say

She's the girl who loves staying true to her inner child
She takes time to color in coloring books or painting a painting
or just finding joy swinging on swings
or just playing video games

She's the girl who has had a few heart breaks
Mostly due to the fact that when she loves, she loves hard
Giving her precious heart to the wrong people
and commiting to people who never committed to her
never seeing the red flags until its too late

She's the girl learning from her mistakes
Using her past traumas as a way to grow
Strengthening her mind, body, and soul
so that when she looks in the mirror she'll sile
not just because of her beauty but also because of her glow

She's the girl writing this so that she can always go back and read it over and over again to remind herselfof the beautiful queen she has become
Despite the communication issues, the "immaturity", and the heart breaks
the girl that chooses to focus on the growth
The girl that's me
